Steps:

  1. Download the bootstrap.dat file
  2. Shutdown your Beyondcoin wallet.
  3. Browse to your Beyondcoin data directory.

    4. Linux

    ~/.beyondcoin

    OSX

    ~/Library/Application\ Support/beyondcoin/

    Windows

    %appdata%\beyondcoin

  4. Make a copy of wallet.dat just in case or backup your private keys.
  5. Delete the folders; blocks and chainstate in your existing Beyondcoin data folder.
  6. Move the bootstrap.dat file to your Beyondcoin data directory.
